Names

Hmm ... difficult.

Low quality individuals have some additional terms attached to their names describing their function: President, Maestro, Engineer, Father etc.

They are less individuals and more Functions. Some robots with a special feature, destination.

The function is stronger than the individual itself, moreover the individual lets, by free will, even more by Desire, the function to take over (a part of) its life.

Since the function lives in many individuals it is stronger than the individual, and the sum of individuals where it is present.

Then comes the real people: John, Levi and Einstein. They are nothing more than their names and they don’t need additional metaphors to become recognizable, part of the world.

Being simple names they have less responsibilities, lighter lives, and more chances to stay agile even when such unforeseen events occur like death, or end of the world.

And finally the lucky ones are identified only by nicknames perfectly describing them: Shorty, Miss Piggy, Jack the Ripper, or Left Eye.

These nicks are perfect because they are surjective: given objectively by friends who know you better in some matters, and, happily accepted by you because it suits you perfectly.

So that’s the meaning of life: find and live your nick.
