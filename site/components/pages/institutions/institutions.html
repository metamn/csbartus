Institutions

Nobody thought the conquer of capitalism over socialism will have a drawback, kickback, revenge.

Here at East everything was institutionalised even life. The revolution, in fact, was about changing these institutions.

Now, after 20 years all Western institutions are napstered or under fire.

It seems the change was not about ideologies but structures, and not from west to east but vice versa.
