Name And Profile

In war under heavy pressure soldiers are asked for

Name and rank
to check their physical integrity. If one fails the identity test he or she will be taken down immediately.

If you don’t know who you are you can be anything or you can be taken over by anyone.

Netizens don’t have rank but sooner or later they will have to have a public profile.
