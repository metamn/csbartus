Digital Samsara

When you spend too much time with computers you’ll loose the sense of reality. Your heart won’t be able anymore to follow your brain.

Next, your attention will become very narrowed allowing you to focus only on a very few people at once and only on very interesting conversations.

With other words you’ll become incompatible with life outside your computer. Nothing offline will satisfy you anymore. Because you’ll have too much speed for this very static slow life.

Humans are far less interesting than creation itself. You can’t create anymore together with your friends.
