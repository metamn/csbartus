Everybody has a goal in life. Some have a strategy to achieve. Few have the ability — tactics — to realise.

Goals worth nothing because anybody can have many. Strategy is put higher — not anybody can do proper planning.

Moreover it turns out planning is obsolete. Plans are not realizable due to their disconnected nature from reality.

Goals, plans and strategy are pure thoughts distorted during execution by the unforeseen.

So it’s all about tactics. And when you build from bottom up — as everything in this century — strategy and goals become useless.

Pure tactics without strategy requires huge life experience, sharp instincts and slim lifestyle. You are caught in the present not worrying — letting to be influenced — about past and future.

A mastery few achieved, or by many but unknown everyday ‘heroes’.
