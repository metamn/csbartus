Today we live the age of tolerance. There are no more moral and theological canons to control the forming of individuals.

Today it is free to construct yourself in a way you are able to do it.

This way the role of Control must be re-examined.

Fortunately the only control exists today is financial, based on consumerism.

If you are a good consumer and tax payer you are ok.

This makes our era one of the nicest and easiest to live in modern history.
